Our science-based approach to net zero is designed to mitigate emissions across our value chain and help address impacts on natural ecosystems, communities and food systems.

At the end of 2023, we:
- Achieved a reduction in emissions of 12.75% vs. 2018 baseline. With removals, a 13.58% net reduction vs. 2018.
- Are more than halfway to our 2025 Net Zero Roadmap target of 20% absolute emissions reductions against a 2018 baseline.
- Identified over 80% of the supply chain initiatives needed to hit this objective.
- Are planning further GHG emissions reductions to reach a 50% cut in absolute terms across our net zero scope by 2030.
